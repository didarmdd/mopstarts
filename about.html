About MopStars

We bring the sparkle, you enjoy the calm

Local, Trusted, & Kiwi Owned

Founded in 2024, MopStars began with a simple idea: cleaning shouldn't just be about removing dirt; it should be about creating healthy, happy environments. We saw a need for a cleaning service in Auckland that was reliable, professional, and environmentally conscious.

What started as a small team with a couple of mops has grown into a full-service cleaning company trusted by homeowners and businesses alike. We take pride in our Kiwi roots and our commitment to excellence.
